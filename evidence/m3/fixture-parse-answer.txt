answer parser (docs/M3-BRIEF.md, free deliverable 3). NOT part of reclaim-eval.

Runs in THIS clone with THIS venv (lossy-wall's D1 wall: the author's code never runs
inside our repo). Feeds `reclaim.llm.parse_answer` the escaped-dollar reply strings
archived verbatim in lossy-wall's evidence (deepseek's LaTeX habit: `ANSWER: \$197`)
plus plain controls, and prints what their parser reads beside what ours read.

Expected mechanism (source-confirmed in src/reclaim/llm.py): `_WRAP` tolerates
markdown/quote wrappers and a literal `$`, but has no backslash — so `ANSWER: \$197`
parses to None, i.e. reads as an abstention. Harmless where the metric is
reclaimed-vs-not (their v2 note documents that logic); it bites where a failure is
decomposed into abstain-vs-emit (their disposition/blank tables).
